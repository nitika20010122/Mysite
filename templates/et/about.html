WHO WE ARE

Et Viridi foundation is an enviromental NGO. Et Viridi Foundation was founded in 2015 and is run by young and Passionate nature lovers. We believe in ACTION by doing as much as we can to preserve our enviroment. sitting idly, complaining, blaming the system, socities or commenting on Internet forums are THINGS WE DO NOT DO.
For effective change we believe in partnerships. We work together.
Increasing the green cover in the city is an important objective and we have planted over 175,000 trees across the city as part of ‘Million Trees Gurgaon’. Waste has been another area of concern, something that is not sustainable at existing magnitudes. We have addressed this through educational campaigns around waste, initiatives led by children and citizens, artist installations to bring awareness amongst others.

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.

Vision

We aim to create a development revolution for the marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.
